Biography


Vesna Longton is a professional artist with a formal art education. She has over 17 years of experience in live event painting.

She paints live at events, including Weddings, Fundraisers, Bar or Bat Mitzvahs, Graduations, Quincea√±eras, Christmas Parties, Corporate Functions. She serves MA, RI, CT, NH, NY, ME and VT.

Vesna was born in 1974 in Moscow, Russia.
1996 Graduated from Moscow State Pedagogical University, Faculty of Fine Art.
1992-1997- Studied fine art at the art studio Old School of Boris Ilyuhin.
1996-1998 - Studied painting at the private art studio of Adronik Yakubyan.
Since 2006 Vesna Longton has lived in Massachusetts USA. She is a freelance artist .
Besides painting live event art , Vesna is an innovative artist who creates three dimensional images on glass and in resin which incorporate the element of light.She also creates sculptures made with elements of resing, colors and lights.
