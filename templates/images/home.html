How It Works

A simple process from booking to finished painting

1

Initial Consultation

We'll discuss your event details, venue, and what moments you'd like captured in your painting. This helps me understand your vision and preferences.

2

Live Painting at Your Event

I'll arrive 1-2 hours before your event to set up in an unobtrusive location. Throughout the event, I'll create a painting that captures the essence and emotions of your special day.

3

Studio Finishing

After your event, I'll take the painting back to my studio to add final details and touches. This ensures your painting is perfect before delivery.

4

Delivery of Your Masterpiece

Your completed painting will be delivered to you, ready to display in your home as a beautiful reminder of your special day for years to come.
